# Vector AI Agent Marketing

## Target customer

- Windows users who want no-code browser automation.
- Operators managing many browser profiles or accounts: SEO agencies, ecommerce teams, lead-gen shops, social media operators, and crypto/Solana users.
- Users who value local execution, free software, and model choice more than enterprise governance.

## Positioning

- Vector positions as the "first autonomous browser operating system" and a free local alternative to Selenium, Puppeteer, Playwright, browser extensions, and cloud automation.
- It emphasizes natural-language commands, unlimited profiles, local storage, no subscriptions, Chrome/Edge control, model-provider choice, and wallet automation.
- The automation page specifically markets use cases such as Solana traders, SEO agencies, ecommerce checkout, lead-gen farms, and creators.

## Captures

- Nontechnical users who are intimidated by browser automation code.
- Growth/agency/crypto users who need account/profile scale and do not want cloud limits.
- Privacy-sensitive users who believe local execution is safer than hosted browser automation.

## Does not capture

- Compliance-heavy teams that need clear abuse controls, audit trails, procurement, and policy enforcement.
- Developers who prefer deterministic Playwright/CDP traces and testable scripts.
- Users on macOS/Linux or teams uncomfortable with aggressive wallet/profile automation claims.
