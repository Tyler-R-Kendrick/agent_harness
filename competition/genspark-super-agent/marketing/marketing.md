# Genspark Super Agent Marketing

## Audience

- Consumers and professionals who want a no-code assistant for research, slides, websites, phone calls, email, and media.
- Small teams that need quick polished deliverables without wiring separate automation tools.
- AI power users looking for a broad workspace that routes across models and tool types.

## Positioning

- "One prompt does it all" is the core message.
- Super Agent is framed as a hybrid system with many models, tools, MCP integrations, and specialized agents.
- The product emphasizes outcome categories over implementation details: research, content creation, analysis, calls, emails, design, and coding.

## Customer Model

- Broad self-serve AI workspace funnel.
- Paid tiers and credit/priority mechanics monetize heavier use.
- Viral workflows such as AI phone calls, fast slide decks, and generated pages drive top-of-funnel awareness.

## Who They Capture

- Users who judge value by finished artifacts rather than inspectable traces.
- Teams that need marketing, sales, research, and operations outputs quickly.
- Non-developers who do not want to understand browser automation, Playwright, MCP, or local runtime state.

## Who They Miss

- Developer and QA teams that need deterministic browser evidence, local screenshots, replay, and regression fixtures.
- Buyers who need to customize the browser execution layer or plug into repo-local extension surfaces.
- Regulated workflows where every source, click, and state-changing action needs a durable audit trail.
