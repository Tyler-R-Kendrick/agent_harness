# AgentSmith Marketing

## Audience

- Individual users and operators tired of repetitive browser tasks.
- Growth, sales, recruiting, job-search, research, and ecommerce users who need scraping or form filling.
- Developers who want MCP browser control but prefer an extension over a full browser-agent stack.

## Positioning

- Core claim: automate clicks, form filling, scraping, and multi-step workflows from natural language.
- Friction wedge: free install, no account, no config files, no API keys, works on Chromium browsers.
- Pricing wedge: simple monthly action quotas instead of credit math.

## Customer Model

- Free daily actions are the acquisition model.
- Starter costs $9.99/month for 1,500 actions/month.
- Pro costs $29.99/month for 4,000 actions/month plus saved workflows and replay.
- Custom tier targets teams needing custom action volume, team management, and dedicated support.

## Who They Capture

- Users who want a visible sidebar and direct task examples rather than a developer runtime.
- Price-sensitive buyers frustrated by token or credit-based agent pricing.
- Power users who want browser MCP as an add-on, not the whole product.

## Who They Miss

- Teams that need deterministic logs, approval policies, replay artifacts, and compliance controls.
- Developers who want open-source internals or self-hosted execution.
- Users who need cross-application OS-level automation, not only browser work.
