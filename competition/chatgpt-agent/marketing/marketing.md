# ChatGPT Agent Marketing

## Audience

- ChatGPT Plus, Pro, Team, Business, Enterprise, and Edu users who want tasks completed without learning automation tools.
- Knowledge workers who move between websites, spreadsheets, documents, calendars, and internal apps.
- Organizations already evaluating ChatGPT as a work operating layer.

## Positioning

- Core frame: ChatGPT can now take action for you, not just answer.
- The browser is positioned as one capability inside a larger work agent.
- Trust is marketed through visible supervision, confirmation prompts, and workspace controls.

## Customer Model

- Subscription-led: agent access is bundled into paid ChatGPT plans with usage limits.
- Enterprise adoption is driven by admin controls, data handling terms, and connectors.
- The product captures existing ChatGPT users before they shop for specialized browser automation.

## Who They Capture

- Mainstream users who want "just do it" task completion.
- Teams already standardizing on ChatGPT.
- Users whose tasks cross browser, files, data analysis, and connectors.

## Who They Miss

- Developers who need deterministic Playwright/CDP-level control, replay files, and checked-in regression evidence.
- Privacy-first users who distrust a hosted model-owner workflow.
- Teams needing a self-hosted or local-first agent surface.
