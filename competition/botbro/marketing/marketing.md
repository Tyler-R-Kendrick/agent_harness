# BotBro Marketing

## Audience

- Consumers and prosumers who want repetitive browser tasks done without writing scripts.
- Operators doing job applications, data entry, scraping, social scheduling, competitor monitoring, price tracking, and restock alerts.
- Users who want automation to run locally and can bring an AI provider key or use a bundled provider.

## Positioning

- Core claim: automate any website privately in plain English.
- Competitive frame: no code, no selectors, no scripts, no recording macros, and no cloud browser dependency.
- Product proof: under-five-minute setup, Windows and macOS downloads, built-in Chromium plus own Chrome, scheduling, SMS alerts, CSV/JSON export, and secure credential storage.

## Customer Model

- Paid consumer app with monthly, annual, and lifetime pricing.
- Single feature set marketed as unlimited automations.
- The plan includes 250 SMS notifications per month and access to multiple AI providers.

## Who They Capture

- Non-developer users who understand browser chores but not automation frameworks.
- Price-sensitive users who prefer a fixed subscription or lifetime purchase over token/credit billing.
- Local-privacy buyers who do not want a cloud service handling credentials.

## Who They Miss

- Developers who need MCP integration, trace files, code APIs, and CI verification.
- Enterprises needing admin controls, SSO, compliance review, and managed audit trails.
- Users who only need occasional one-off browsing and will not pay $25 per month.
