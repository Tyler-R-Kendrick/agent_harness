# Salesforce Agentforce Gossip

## Positive Signals

- Salesforce practitioners recognize the value of Data 360 and Salesforce metadata as the real moat for agents that need customer context.
- AgentExchange gives the ecosystem a familiar route to package, certify, and sell agents around existing AppExchange habits.
- Contact-center coverage gets attention because AI and human agents can share CRM context, transcripts, routing, and analytics.

## Negative Signals

- Community criticism often says Agentforce shipped ahead of maturity, changed shape repeatedly, and relies heavily on Salesforce's broader data platform rather than standalone agent quality.
- Pricing discussions are confused by per-user licenses, conversation pricing, Flex Credits, and which builder/runtime actions consume credits.
- Naming churn is a recurring complaint: AppExchange, AgentExchange, Slack Marketplace, Agentforce 360, Agentforce Contact Center, and related products blur together.

## Bug And Friction Themes

- Accurate estimates require understanding conversation/action volume and Data 360 usage before the organization has production experience.
- Agent quality depends on clean Salesforce data, well-scoped topics, explicit actions, and trusted permission design.
- Marketplace publishing and security review can be useful but adds time, cost, and documentation burden for ISVs.
