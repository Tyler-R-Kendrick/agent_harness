# Gumloop Marketing

## Audience

- Marketing, sales, RevOps, SEO, research, and internal operations teams.
- Non-technical operators who want AI-heavy workflows without writing code.
- Automation agencies and builders selling AI workflow outcomes to small and mid-market businesses.

## Positioning

- Core frame: build powerful AI automations without code.
- Gumloop is positioned as more AI-native than classic workflow tools because reasoning is distributed through the workflow rather than isolated in one generic automation step.
- Agents are marketed as practical work runners that can use web, app, data, and workflow tools.

## Customer Model

- Subscription plans include monthly credits.
- Heavy use is metered through credits for AI model calls, workflow executions, agent interactions, tools, and enrichment operations.
- BYOK discounts appeal to cost-sensitive technical teams and agencies.

## Who They Capture

- Teams that want quick AI workflow prototyping with enough structure to become recurring automation.
- Operators who find n8n too technical or Zapier too linear for AI-heavy work.
- Users who care more about outcome automation than direct browser control.

## Who They Miss

- Developers who need Playwright/CDP-level reproducibility and checked-in browser traces.
- Security-conscious teams that need local-first browser state and explicit session authorization.
- Users whose task requires authenticated visual browsing rather than web fetch or integration APIs.
