# n8n AI Agents Marketing

## Audience

- Developers, technical operators, RevOps teams, and automation agencies.
- Teams that want open-source or self-hostable workflow automation.
- Organizations that need to combine AI with API integrations, code, and approvals.

## Positioning

- Core frame: workflow automation meets AI agents that can run predictably in production.
- n8n positions itself against simpler no-code tools by offering code, self-hosting, and more control.
- AI is marketed as a layer inside workflow automation rather than a replacement for the workflow engine.

## Customer Model

- Free open-source self-hosting creates bottom-up adoption.
- n8n Cloud monetizes managed hosting, executions, team features, and AI Workflow Builder credits.
- Technical teams can bring their own model/provider patterns through AI nodes and integrations.

## Who They Capture

- Engineering-led teams that hit Zapier or Make limits.
- Users who need visible execution logs, custom code, and self-hosted credentials.
- Automation agencies selling durable workflows to clients.

## Who They Miss

- Mainstream users who want a browser assistant rather than a workflow editor.
- Teams that need visual page replay, screenshots, and direct browser state.
- Buyers who do not want to maintain workflow graphs or self-hosted infrastructure.
