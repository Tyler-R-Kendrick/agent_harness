# Prozio AI Marketing

## Audience

- Makers, creators, growth teams, SEO operators, data-entry teams, and small businesses that want browser automation without writing Playwright scripts.
- Users who need to scrape, summarize, monitor competitors, audit websites, fill forms, or push browser data to Sheets, Slack, Notion, Zapier, or an API.
- Buyers who want natural-language agent setup but still need some workflow structure.

## Positioning

- Prozio positions itself as no-code AI browser automation: agents operate in real browsers and workflows integrate with apps.
- The strongest product claims are saved browser profiles, protected sensitive information, multi-agent orchestration, simulated browsers, no credit system, and BYOK.
- The pricing page frames usage around automation runs and playground runs rather than opaque model credits.

## Customer Model

- Free plan for testing, paid individual/team tiers, and business tier with unlimited run language plus abuse monitoring.
- BYOK reduces platform model-cost risk but shifts token-cost understanding to the customer.
- Money-back guarantee and approachable copy target self-serve buyers rather than enterprise procurement first.

## Who They Capture

- Users who have repetitive web tasks and want a hosted no-code workflow before hiring an automation engineer.
- SEO and research workflows where the output is extracted data or a report.
- Teams that want webhooks and API-style triggering without building browser infrastructure.

## Who They Miss

- Developers who want transparent Playwright/CDP scripts and source-controlled test suites.
- Privacy-sensitive local-first users who do not want browser profiles and sensitive task data in a hosted product.
- Enterprises that need formal approvals, audit logs, policy enforcement, redaction rules, and compliance reviews before using saved sessions.

## Competitive Notes For agent-browser

- Prozio's saved profiles and sensitive-info story are directly relevant to local-agent UX.
- `agent-browser` can compete by making those same authority boundaries more visible: what profile is used, what secrets are available, which actions need approval, and what evidence remains after the run.
