# Fellou Gossip

## Positive Signals

- Fellou's official pages and launch press frame the product around deep search, cross-webpage operations, intelligent perception, asynchronous collaboration, and editable execution plans.
- BusinessWire launch copy says Fellou CE introduced a "spatial" agentic browser and claims more than 1,000,000 users since April 2025.
- Reviewers like the plan/credit-estimate pattern because it gives users a better sense of what the agent will do before spending time or credits.

## Negative Signals

- TechCrunch coverage found during this run was sponsored content, so it is useful for positioning but weak as independent validation.
- Security commentary flags prompt-injection risk when untrusted webpage content is forwarded into an agent prompt that can act with user privileges.
- Hands-on reviews report that Fellou works better on simple structured pages than complex layouts, can loop, and cannot bypass CAPTCHA.

## Bug And UX Risk Themes

- Autonomous browsing widens the blast radius from "wrong answer" to "wrong action."
- Local files, memory, logged-in platforms, and desktop operations require unusually clear permission boundaries.
- Credit pricing can make retries and slow failed tasks feel expensive.
