# UiPath Agentic Automation Marketing

## Audience

- Enterprise automation centers of excellence.
- CIOs, operations leaders, finance, public sector, manufacturing, and regulated teams.
- Existing RPA customers looking for an agentic upgrade path without abandoning governance.

## Positioning

- UiPath positions itself as the agentic business orchestration platform.
- The core message is that agentic AI needs deterministic orchestration, human leadership, and enterprise guardrails.
- The platform is pitched as open and interoperable: build UiPath agents, third-party agents, UI automations, and API workflows together.

## Customer Model

- Free trial/community entry for exploration.
- Enterprise SaaS through Automation Cloud plus self-hosting for organizations with control or compliance requirements.
- Expansion motion through centers of excellence, process discovery, document understanding, testing, and line-of-business use cases.

## Captures

- Buyers that already trust UiPath for RPA and want to add agents without losing controls.
- Teams with enough process volume to justify suite-level governance.
- Compliance-heavy workflows where audit and human approval matter more than consumer-grade browsing.

## Misses

- Small teams that want cheap browser-agent primitives.
- Developers who want local-first state, transparent traces, and direct code-level control instead of platform workflow design.
- End users who want an AI browser experience rather than a managed enterprise automation program.
