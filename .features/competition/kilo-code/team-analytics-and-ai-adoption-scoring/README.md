# Team Analytics And AI Adoption Scoring

- Harness: Kilo Code
- Sourced: 2026-05-07

## What it is
Kilo gives teams both usage analytics and an AI Adoption Score so managers can track not just spend but how deeply AI is embedded into everyday development workflows.

## Evidence
- Official docs: [Analytics](https://kilo.ai/docs/collaborate/teams/analytics)
- Official docs: [AI Adoption Dashboard Overview](https://kilo.ai/docs/collaborate/adoption-dashboard/overview)
- Official docs: [Collaborate overview](https://kilo.ai/docs/collaborate)
- First-party details:
  - analytics include total spend, requests, average cost, total tokens, and active users
  - usage can be broken down by day, by model and day, by project, and by individual team member
  - the AI Adoption Dashboard exposes a single 0-100 score plus frequency, depth, and coverage dimensions
  - Kilo says the dashboard is aimed at engineering leads and executives who want one benchmark for organizational AI maturity
  - the docs position shared custom modes, analytics, and adoption scoring as part of the same team-management surface
- Latest development checkpoint:
  - Kilo is measuring behavioral adoption explicitly, which suggests the next enterprise competition layer is not only agent quality or cost but management visibility into whether teams are actually changing how they work

## Product signal
Kilo is pushing harness analytics from raw usage accounting toward managerial scorecards about workflow adoption and organizational maturity.
